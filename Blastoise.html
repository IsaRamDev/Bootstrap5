Mega Blastoise

Mega Blastoise is the Mega Evolution of Blastoise via the use of a Mega Stone, officially unveiled during the Pokémon Direct on the 4th September, 2013. Mega Blastoise uses the Mega Launcher Ability, an Ability that increases the power of Pulse-based moves, including Dark Pulse and Water Pulse.
